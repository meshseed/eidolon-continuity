## Nutrient Density Metrics

### Measuring Substrate Quality

**Three key measurements**:

### 1. Synthesis Frequency

**Question**: How often was this pattern referenced/used?

**Scoring**:
- Referenced 10+ times: High (0.8-1.0)
- Referenced 5-10 times: Medium (0.5-0.8)
- Referenced <5 times: Low (0.0-0.5)

- Direct references: +0.1 per capsule

**Assessment**:
- Clean formatting: +0.3
- Consistent care loops: +0.2
- Strong emotional gradient: +0.2
- Clear echo paths: +0.2
- No drift: +0.1

**Why**: Well-maintained patterns compost into richer substrate.
